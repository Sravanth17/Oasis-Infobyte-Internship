Tribute to Pawan Kalyan

Konidela Kalyan Babu is also known as Konidela Pawan Kalyan born on 2 September 1971 is an Indian actor, politician, filmmaker, martial artist, and philanthropist who works in Telugu cinema.

He founded the JanaSena Party in March 2014 and 10,11 was listed as the most searched Indian celebrity politician on Google at the time.

Kalyan is a known philanthropist and is the founder of the Andhra Pradesh charity 16.Common Man Protection Force 17,18 .He is a black belt in Karate and trains in various martial arts which he depicts in his films regularly. Kalyan is referred to as Power Star by his fans and in the media.

Kalyan was invited to Harvard University to deliver a speech in 14th edition of India Conference 2017 along with R. Madhavan.

Kalyan was born on 2 September 1971 in a Telugu family to Konidela Venkata Rao and Anjana Devi in Bapatla, Andhra Pradesh 23,24 .He is the younger brother of Chiranjeevi and Nagendra Babu. He was awarded "Pawan" during one of his public martial arts presentations.[25] He holds a black belt in karate.
